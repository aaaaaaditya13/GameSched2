1. First Come First Serve (FCFS) - Default Algorithm

How it Works:

  • ‚Ä¢ Processes execute in arrival order
  • ‚Ä¢ Non-preemptive - no interruptions
  • ‚Ä¢ Each entity gets their full time slice
  • ‚Ä¢ Fair but can feel slow
Gaming Impact: Turn-based movement - wait for your turn to move

What You'll Experience:

‚Ä¢ Player and enemies take turns
‚Ä¢ Each gets their full time slice
‚Ä¢ Movement blocked when not your turn
‚Ä¢ Predictable turn order

This is the permanent default - all other algorithms are temporary!

2. Power-ups & Temporary Algorithms (3 seconds only)

Available Algorithms:

  • ‚Ä¢ Round Robin: Fixed time slices with preemption
  • ‚Ä¢ SJF/SRTF: Shortest job/remaining time first
  • ‚Ä¢ Priority (Non-Preemptive): Priority order, no interruption
  • ‚Ä¢ Priority (Preemptive): Priority with interruption
Important: All non-FCFS algorithms last exactly 3 seconds, then revert to FCFS

How to Activate:

‚Ä¢ Collect yellow power-up circles
‚Ä¢ Or select from dropdown menu
‚Ä¢ Timer shows remaining time
‚Ä¢ Automatic revert to FCFS after 3s

Strategy: Use algorithms wisely during critical moments!

3. Difficulty Levels & Lives System

Difficulty Affects Time Slices:

  • ‚Ä¢ Easy: 2.9 seconds per turn (more time to think)
  • ‚Ä¢ Normal: 1.9 seconds per turn (balanced)
  • ‚Ä¢ Hard: 1.0 second per turn + 1 boss
  • ‚Ä¢ Super Hard: 1.0 second per turn + 2 bosses
Lives System: 3 lives total. Lose a life when touching enemies. Get first move priority after respawn.

Priority Values:

Priority 1: Player (highest)
Priority 2: Bosses
Priority 3: Regular Enemies (lowest)

Priority matters most during Priority scheduling algorithms
